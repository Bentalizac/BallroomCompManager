# Architecture Decision Record (ADR)

## 1. Title
Short name describing the decision (e.g., "Adopt Domain-Driven Design for Shared Types")

## 2. Status
- Proposed / Accepted / Deprecated / Superseded

## 3. Context
Explain the problem or need:

- Current architecture limitations  
- Team goals or constraints  
- External requirements or dependencies  

---

## 4. Decision
Clearly state the architecture choice made:

- Chosen design pattern, module structure, or approach  
- Specific technologies or frameworks  
- How components interact (high-level overview)  

---

## 5. Alternatives Considered
- Option A: pros/cons  
- Option B: pros/cons  
- Option C: pros/cons  

Explain why the chosen decision is preferred.

---

## 6. Consequences
- Positive effects (improved maintainability, scalability, clarity)  
- Potential tradeoffs (learning curve, temporary complexity, refactor effort)  
- Impact on future features or schema changes  

---

## 7. Implementation Notes
- Files/modules affected  
- Required migrations or setup  
- Dependencies to install or modify  

---

## 8. Related Decisions
- Links to other ADRs, feature docs, or refactor proposals  

---

## 9. Open Questions
- Decisions left for future discussion  
- Known unknowns  

---

## 10. Checklist
- [ ] Team agrees on decision  
- [ ] Documentation updated  
- [ ] RAG embeddings refreshed
